Privacy Policy

MAP Accounting — Practice Integration App · Last updated: 8 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MAP Accounting ("we", "us", "our") handles personal data when our Practice Integration App (the "App") connects to a client's QuickBooks Online account. We are the data controller for the purposes of the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. What data we access

Through Intuit's QuickBooks Online API, the App may access accounting records of the authorising company, including supplier and customer names, transactions, invoices, expenses, VAT data and company financial information. Some of this may constitute personal data (for example, names and contact details of sole traders, directors or contacts).

2. Why we use it (lawful basis)

We process this data to deliver the accounting, bookkeeping, VAT and management-accounts services our clients have engaged us to provide. Our lawful bases are the performance of our contract with the client and our legitimate interests in operating the practice efficiently, as well as compliance with our legal and regulatory obligations.

3. How we store and protect it

Access tokens are stored securely and access to client data is limited to authorised members of the firm. We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect data against unauthorised access, loss or disclosure. We do not sell client data or use it for advertising.

4. Sharing

We do not share QuickBooks data with third parties except: (a) Intuit, as the provider of QuickBooks Online; (b) HMRC and other authorities where required to provide our services or by law; and (c) trusted service providers acting on our instructions under appropriate data-protection terms.

5. Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services and to meet legal, accounting and regulatory record-keeping requirements, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

6. Your rights

Individuals have rights under the UK GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and portability. A client may revoke the App's access to their QuickBooks data at any time through their Intuit account. To exercise any right, contact us using the details below.

7. Complaints

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
